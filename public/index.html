Welcome to Qira'at al-Quran Institute

At Qira'at al-Quran Institute, we nurture a deep connection with the Quran through rigorous instruction in the sacred science of Tajwid and Qira'at. Our mission is to empower students with the art of Quranic recitation, fostering excellence and preserving the authentic traditions of Quranic study.

Join our diverse community of students from around the world, including the UK, USA, Canada, Malaysia, Qatar, Zambia, South Africa, India, and Zimbabwe, in our commitment to spiritual growth, academic excellence, and a profound understanding of the Quran.
